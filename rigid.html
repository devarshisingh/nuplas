Laminated PVC / PE Film

Our ‘Food Grade’ Film is a laminated PVC-PE multi-layer film for thermoforming. It is made using a special non-toxic formula and a solvent-free process, ensuring the sanitation of food packaging. This material has excellent forming ability and impact resistance, making it ideal for food packaging.

Key Features
  • Suitable for packaging food
  • Excellent oxygen and moisture resistance. Helps prevent the formation of gas and odor while keeping food fresh for several days.
  • Color: Clear and Opaque options.
  • PE Film thickness: 50 microns.
  • Compliance: Film conforms to EU specifications 90/128/EEC (Amendment as 2002/72/EC) and 78/142/EEC. Certificate can be provided upon request.
