About Us

Nuplas Industries Ltd. is established in 1997 at Jebel Ali Free Zone near Dubai city of the United Arab Emirates as a manufacturer and primary and secondary packaging material supplier to pharmaceutical, food, and FMCG industries. We invested in high-standard European calendaring machines for PVC film and its base products. The aim was to deliver packaging solutions to manufacturers of food and drugs in and around the Middle East.

As we evolved into becoming one of the largest manufacturers of primary packaging material like RIGID PVC FILMS in the region, we expanded our footprints beyond the Middle East with warehousing and distribution facilities in West and South Africa. Nuplas, with an in-depth understanding of their pharmaceutical and food customer’s packaging requirements, also regularly upgrades manufacturing standards to ensure that we become a supplier with complete regulatory compliance as per international standards.
